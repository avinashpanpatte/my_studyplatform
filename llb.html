Constitutional Law

Fundamental rights, directive principles, and constitutional framework.

  • Meaning of Constitution and basic structure doctrine
  • Fundamental Rights: equality, freedom, religion, remedies
  • DPSP and difference between Rights vs DPSP
  • Writs: Habeas Corpus, Mandamus, Certiorari, Prohibition, Quo Warranto
📝
Contract Law

Offer, acceptance, consideration, breach, and legal remedies.

  • Essentials of valid contract under Indian Contract Act
  • Offer and acceptance with communication rules
  • Consideration and free consent (coercion, fraud, misrepresentation)
  • Breach of contract and available legal remedies
⚖️
Criminal Law

General principles, offences, punishment, and criminal procedure basics.

  • Actus reus and mens rea in criminal liability
  • Common offences: theft, cheating, hurt, homicide
  • Stages of crime: intention, preparation, attempt, commission
  • Bail, FIR, arrest and trial basics in criminal procedure
📚
Jurisprudence

Schools of legal thought, legal reasoning, and theory of law.

  • Nature, meaning and scope of jurisprudence
  • Schools: Natural, Analytical, Historical, Sociological
  • Concepts of rights, duties, possession and ownership
  • Justice and legal reasoning in landmark case analysis
